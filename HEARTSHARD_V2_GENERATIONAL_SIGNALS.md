## ðŸŒ€ Generational Signal Mechanics

### How It Works

1. **Character enters the Maw**
   - Their journey concludes in this generation
   - They become part of the sealing/healing

2. **Birth signal generated**
   - Location: Current polarity state
   - Generation: Parent's generation + 1
   - Lineage: Tracks all ancestor names

3. **"Similar salt" calculation**
   - Find nearby polarity states (flip 0-2 axes)
   - Creates neighborhood of related states
   - Example: `(+1, -1, +1)` â†’ might become `(-1, -1, +1)` or `(+1, +1, +1)`

4. **Next generation spawn**
   - Character re-emerges in similar polarity region
   - New bonds form based on proximity in polarity space
   - Feels like their child inherited their essence

5. **Playthrough increments**
   - World reshuffles
   - Timeline branches
   - Same entities, different configuration

### Why This Is Brilliant

**Traditional games:** Reset button. You lose progress. Feels bad.

**HeartShard:** "Death" is **transformation**. Your hero's essence lives on. Their signal spawns someone similar but new. Every ending births a new generation.

**It solves the Chrono Trigger problem:** Never runs out of endings because each ending generates new starting conditions via signals.
